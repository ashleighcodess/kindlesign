FAQ

LED Sign Questions

Common questions about LED and digital signage. We're happy to explain more.

866-358-9892
LED sign pricing depends on size, resolution, and features. Basic message centers start around $5,000-$10,000. Full-color displays range from $15,000 to $100,000+ depending on size and pixel pitch. We offer financing options.
LED sign regulations vary by municipality. Most areas allow them but may have restrictions on brightness, animation, and placement. We research local codes as part of our consultation and handle all permitting.
Most LED signs include software for easy content updates from any computer or mobile device. You can schedule messages, create animations, and manage multiple signs remotely. We provide full training.
Our LED signs feature automatic brightness adjustment based on ambient light. They're visible in direct sunlight and dim appropriately at night to avoid light pollution and comply with local ordinances.
We offer 5-year warranties on our LED displays covering parts and labor. Extended warranty and maintenance plans are available. LED modules are designed to last 100,000+ hours of operation.

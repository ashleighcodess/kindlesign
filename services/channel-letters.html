Channel letter pricing varies based on size, quantity, illumination type, and installation complexity. Most projects range from $3,000 to $15,000+. Front-lit letters are typically most affordable, while combination front and back-lit options cost more. We provide detailed quotes after assessing your specific needs.
With proper installation and quality materials, channel letters last 10-15+ years. LED modules typically offer 50,000+ hours of operation, which translates to many years of reliable service. Kindle Sign Company offers a 5-year warranty on all channel letter installations.
Yes, most municipalities require sign permits for channel letters. Kindle Sign Company handles all permitting for Bucks County, Montgomery County, Philadelphia, and surrounding areas in PA, NJ, DE, and FL as part of our turnkey service.
Typical channel letter projects take 4-6 weeks from design approval to installation. Rush orders may be available depending on complexity. Permitting timelines vary by municipality and can add 2-4 weeks.
Front-lit channel letters illuminate through translucent letter faces, providing maximum brightness and visibility. Halo-lit (backlit) channel letters project light behind the letters against the wall, creating an elegant glow effect. Combination letters offer both for maximum impact.

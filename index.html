FAQ

Questions About Custom Signs?

Common questions from businesses in PA, NJ, DE & FL.

Ask Us
Kindle Sign Company serves the East Coast with offices in Langhorne, PA and Stuart, FL. We provide sign services throughout Pennsylvania (Bucks, Montgomery, Chester, Delaware, Lehigh, Northampton, Berks counties), New Jersey (Burlington, Camden, Mercer, Monmouth, Ocean and more), Delaware (New Castle, Kent counties), and Florida (Martin, Palm Beach, Broward, Miami-Dade, Brevard counties).
We specialize in channel letters, monument signs, pylon signs, LED digital signs, awnings and canopies, vehicle wraps, dimensional letters, wayfinding signage, and complete branding packages. All signs are custom designed and fabricated in-house.
Project timelines vary by sign type. Simple signs take 2-3 weeks, channel letters take 4-6 weeks, and monument signs take 8-12 weeks including permitting. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and keep you informed throughout the process.
Yes, Kindle Sign Company handles all permitting as part of our turnkey service. We manage permits in Bucks County, Montgomery County, Philadelphia, and all municipalities we serve in PA, NJ, DE, and FL.
Sign costs depend on type, size, materials, and installation complexity. Channel letters typically range from $3,000-$15,000+, monument signs from $5,000-$25,000+, and pylon signs from $10,000-$50,000+. Contact us for a free quote tailored to your specific needs.
Yes, we provide complete turnkey service from design through installation. Our licensed, insured installation crews handle all mounting, electrical connections, and final inspections. We serve customers from our Langhorne PA and Stuart FL offices.
